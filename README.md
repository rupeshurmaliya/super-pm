# Super PM: The High-Pressure Interview Simulator

**Super PM** is a specialized Gemini CLI extension built for those who don't just want to "practice" PM interviews—they want to master them. It transforms your terminal into a high-stakes interview room led by a "tough but fair" 10-year veteran Senior Product Leader.

---

## 🎯 Who is this for?

**Super PM** is designed for high-growth individuals who need more than a generic AI chat:

*   **Aspiring PMs:** Candidates preparing for FAANG or Tier-1 startup interviews who need to move beyond memorized frameworks and practice first-principles thinking.
*   **Senior PMs & Leads:** Experienced professionals looking to sharpen their strategic depth or practice handling high-pressure stakeholder pivots.
*   **Interview Prep Groups:** Use the simulator as a "live" candidate while others observe and critique the meta-commentary provided by the AI.
*   **Hiring Managers:** Use the question seeds and rubrics as a benchmark for what "Great" looks like in a candidate response.

## 💡 Why use Super PM?

Standard AI interactions are often too helpful or collaborative. **Super PM** is intentionally designed to be a "friction-heavy" environment:

1.  **Ambiguity by Design:** You won't get a clean prompt. You'll get a vague objective like "Fix our conversion rate," forcing you to drive the discovery process.
2.  **Zero Premature Validation:** The interviewer won't tell you "That's a great idea." They will ask, "Why did you prioritize that segment over the three others you missed?"
3.  **The Mid-Interview Pivot:** Just when you think you have a solid roadmap, the interviewer will cut your budget by 70% or introduce a new regulation, testing your ability to adapt under pressure.
4.  **Meta-Commentary:** Between phases, you get "off-the-record" feedback on your performance, giving you immediate insight into your communication and logical gaps.

## 📖 How to Use

### 1. Starting a Session
Activate the skill and choose your challenge:
*   **Select a Track:** Product Sense (Design), Execution (Metrics), or Strategy.
*   **Select an Industry:** Fintech, EdTech, InsurTech, or General.
*   **Engage:** The interviewer will present an ambiguous prompt. Your job is to take control, ask clarifying questions, and build a structured response.

### 4. The Evaluation
At the end of the session, you will receive a comprehensive breakdown based on professional hiring rubrics, covering:
*   **Product Vision:** Can you see the big picture?
*   **Strategic Thinking:** Do you understand the "Why" and the "Now"?
*   **Analytical Rigor:** Can you defend your metrics and trade-offs?
*   **Communication:** How effectively do you handle pressure and skepticism?
