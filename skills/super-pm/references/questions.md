# Product Management Interview Question Bank

## 1. Product Sense / Design

### Fintech
- Design a cross-border remittance app for users in emerging markets.
- How would you improve the mortgage application process for first-time homebuyers?
- Design a "buy now, pay later" feature for a major e-commerce platform.

### EdTech
- Design a language learning app for elderly users.
- How would you improve the experience of remote learning for primary school students?
- Design a platform that connects retired professionals with students for mentorship.

### Insurtech
- Design a usage-based auto insurance product for low-mileage drivers.
- How would you simplify the health insurance claim process using mobile technology?
- Design a micro-insurance product for small-scale farmers in developing nations.

### General
- Design a better alarm clock for the 21st century.
- How would you improve the experience of finding a public restroom in a large city?
- Design a smart refrigerator for a shared apartment.

## 2. Execution / Metrics

### Fintech
- A digital bank sees a 15% drop in credit card applications after launching a new UI. How do you diagnose?
- What metrics would you track to measure the success of a new peer-to-peer payment feature?
- We want to increase the number of users who set up recurring transfers. How would you approach this?

### EdTech
- Our 'Premium Study Plan' conversion dropped by 12% after we introduced a social-learning feature. How do you diagnose?
- How would you measure the "learning efficacy" of a new interactive quiz feature?
- Engagement on our mobile app is high, but web completion rates are low. Why might this be happening?

### Insurtech
- The number of incomplete insurance quotes has risen by 20% this month. What's your investigation plan?
- How would you measure the impact of a new AI-driven underwriting engine on customer satisfaction?
- We want to reduce the "time to claim settlement." What are the key trade-offs to consider?

### General
- YouTube's "Watch Later" usage is down 5%. How do you investigate?
- What metrics would you use to measure the success of a new "Night Mode" feature on a social media app?
- How would you decide whether to prioritize a new feature or fix existing bugs?

## 3. Product Strategy

### Fintech
- Should a traditional bank launch its own crypto-trading platform or partner with an existing exchange?
- How would you compete against a new entrant offering "zero-fee" international transfers?
- What is the long-term strategy for a "super-app" in the financial services space?

### EdTech
- A major university wants to move all its degree programs online. What's your strategy?
- How should a language learning app respond to the rise of real-time AI translation tools?
- Should an EdTech startup focus on B2B (schools) or B2C (students) in the long run?

### Insurtech
- You are a PM at a major insurer. A startup is offering on-demand 'Gig Economy' insurance. Should you build a competing product, buy them, or partner?
- How can insurance companies use IoT data to move from "reimbursement" to "prevention"?
- Should a home insurance company expand into the "home security" hardware market?

### General
- Should Amazon launch its own physical pharmacy chain?
- If you were the PM for Google Maps, how would you monetize the product without degrading the user experience?
- How should Netflix respond to the increasing fragmentation of the streaming market?

## 4. Behavioral / Leadership

### General
- Tell me about a time you had to make a difficult decision without having all the data.
- Describe a situation where you had a conflict with an engineer or designer. How did you resolve it?
- Tell me about a product you failed to launch. What did you learn?
- How do you handle a situation where your team is behind schedule and a key stakeholder is demanding more features?
- Tell me about a time you had to influence someone who didn't report to you.
- What is the most complex technical challenge you've had to explain to a non-technical stakeholder?
